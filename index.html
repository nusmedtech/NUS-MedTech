Why MedTech?
Our Purpose

Founded in 2019, NUS MedTech is a vibrant student society empowering NUS students to design and drive healthcare innovations for a better future.


Our mission: To create a dynamic platform for learning, problem-solving, and exploring real-world biomedical engineering applications beyond the classroom.

Connect with Us

Throughout the year, members can engage in project recruitment, talks, workshops, events, and competitions. NUS MedTech is also producing educational videos on the MedTech industry. By participating, members will have the opportunity to connect with industry speakers and healthcare professionals, compete in valuable competitions, and gain experiences that can boost their careers and open doors to internships.
